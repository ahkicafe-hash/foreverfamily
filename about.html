ABOUT FOREVER FAMILY

WHO WE
ARE.

Three bodies. One mission. A community-led infrastructure built to protect, train, and respond — in the places and moments others won't.

THE PIPELINE — HOW WE WORK
STAGE 01

PIA

The Public Infrastructure Authority is the standards body of Forever Family. PIA sets the code of conduct, certification criteria, and the 12-Point assessment framework that every Stepper must complete before deployment.

STANDARDS CERTIFICATION ACCOUNTABILITY
WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU

Every member and Stepper operates under PIA-certified standards. When you join Forever Family, you're joining an organisation with a documented code — not a loose collective.

STAGE 02

FFCA

The Forever Family Community Academy is where the training happens. FFCA delivers the 12-Point Programme — 60 assessments across 12 disciplines — turning community members into certified Steppers.

TRAINING 12-POINT PROGRAMME 60 ASSESSMENTS
OPEN MONTHLY

FFCA sessions run monthly and are open to Bronze members and above. Structured, practical, and held in secure community venues. Theory meets street reality.

STAGE 03

SOS

The Safe Operations Squad is the deployment arm. Certified Steppers conduct community safety patrols, crisis interventions, and G-Line responses in active operational zones across the UK.

DEPLOYMENT CRISIS RESPONSE PATROLS
CLASSIFIED OPERATIONS

SOS operations are disclosed to Silver members and above. Not everything we do can be public — the communities we protect come first, always.

STAGE 04

COMMUNITY

This is where the mission lands. Every session attended, every patrol logged, every G-Line response answered — it all flows back here. Safer streets. Stronger young people. Families kept intact.
